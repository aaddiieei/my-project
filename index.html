Delhi floods.
We see it coming.

A GIS-based flood risk engine for urban India.

Every monsoon, large parts of Delhi go underwater. Low-lying wards, clogged drains, and rising rainfall events leave millions vulnerable — yet most interventions happen only after the damage is done.

HydroSense predicts risk before it rains. By combining terrain elevation, drainage capacity proxies, and rainfall intensity into a weighted score, we identify the exact localities most likely to flood — ward by ward.

Actionable, visual, and scalable. Results are plotted live on an interactive map — so civic authorities can prioritise drainage repair, pre-position pumps, and issue ward-level alerts days in advance.
